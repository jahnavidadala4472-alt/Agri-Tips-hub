# Agri-Tips-hub
Agri Tips Hub is a smart and user-friendly digital platform designed to support farmers with modern agricultural knowledge and practical farming guidance. The website provides valuable tips, crop-wise information, and expert assistance to help farmers improve productivity and make better farming decisions.
It includes detailed sections on various crops such as rice, wheat, maize, tomato, chilli, and more, along with recommended fertilizers, chemical usage ratios, and water requirements. Farmers can easily explore useful tips that open directly in Google for deeper learning and watch farming-related videos that open directly on YouTube.

The platform also features an AI-powered assistant that answers farmers’ queries in both English and Telugu, making it accessible and easy to use. Additionally, it provides complete information about important government schemes, including benefits, eligibility, and direct application links.

With a clean green-themed design, high-quality images, and mobile-friendly interface, Agri Tips Hub acts as a one-stop solution for farmers to gain knowledge, solve problems, and enhance their agricultural practices.
